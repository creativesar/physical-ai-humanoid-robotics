## Why Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics?

Physical AI represents a fundamental shift from traditional AI systems that operate purely in digital spaces to AI that can interact with and manipulate the physical world. Humanoid robotics, in particular, focuses on creating robots with human-like form and capabilities, enabling natural interaction with human environments and tools.

This field is crucial for:
- **Assistive Technologies**: Helping elderly and disabled individuals maintain independence
- **Industrial Automation**: Collaborative robots that work safely alongside humans
- **Service Industries**: Robots that can navigate and operate in human spaces
- **Research**: Advancing our understanding of intelligence and embodiment
