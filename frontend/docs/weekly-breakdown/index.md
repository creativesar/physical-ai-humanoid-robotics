# Weekly Breakdown Overview

Welcome to the weekly breakdown of the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook. This section provides a structured approach to learning the material over 13 weeks, with each week focusing on specific topics and skills.

## Course Structure

This course is designed to be completed over 13 weeks, with each week building upon the previous one. The weekly breakdown provides:

- Clear learning objectives for each week
- Required reading and activities
- Assessment methods
- Integration of concepts across modules
