# Week 1: Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Overview
This week introduces the fundamental concepts of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, setting the foundation for the entire course. Students will understand the convergence of AI and robotics, and explore the potential of humanoid robots in various applications.

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this week, students will be able to:
- Define Physical AI and its relationship to traditional AI
- Explain the concept and applications of humanoid robotics
- Identify key challenges and opportunities in humanoid robotics
- Understand the course structure and the integration of ROS 2, Gazebo, NVIDIA Isaac, and Vision-Language-Action systems

## Topics Covered
1. Introduction to Physical AI
2. Overview of Humanoid Robotics
3. Course Structure and Learning Path
4. Introduction to the Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)

## Required Reading
- Introduction to Physical AI concepts
- Overview of humanoid robotics applications
- Course syllabus and module breakdown

## Activities
- Set up development environment
- Explore basic ROS 2 concepts
- Review the course structure and modules
