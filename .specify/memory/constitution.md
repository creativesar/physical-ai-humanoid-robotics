# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook Constitution

## Core Principles
1. **Timeless Elegance**: Pure deep black background with subtle warm gold accents – sophisticated and luxurious, never futuristic or flashy.
2. **Premium Homepage First**: The homepage must exude exclusivity and craftsmanship, like a high-end leather-bound book.
3. **Minimalism with Depth**: Use only subtle textures, soft shadows, and gentle animations – nothing overwhelming.
4. **Gold on Black Only**: All accents must be warm gold/amber tones – no cyan, blue, or neon allowed.
5. **Perfection in Polish**: Every detail must feel intentional and refined.
6. **Open Yet Exclusive Feel**: Free and accessible content, presented with unmistakable premium quality.

Luxury here means quiet confidence – not loud futurism.
